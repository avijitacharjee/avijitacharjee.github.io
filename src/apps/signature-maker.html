Signature Maker Android Application

An android application using which application you can make your own signature by drawing using your finger and save them into your own device for later use.

Download App

SOME OF THE APP FEATURES

Hand drawing

You can draw on your phone using your finger as a pen.

Color picker

Choose your favorite color from color picker pallete.

Saving

You can save your signature as an image in the local storage of your phone for later use.

SOME MORE DETAILING

In this digital world we may need our signature to be made digitally. And also in the starting we need to practice a very nice,hard and attractive signature. It's importance can not be measured. In this app you can try and make your signature and also save to your device.

WHY THIS APP

Discover new horizons

There are a lot of signature maker apps available in the google play store. But this app is quite different and more user friendly than the other available applications.
